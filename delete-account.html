Delete Your Account

Expedoo — Account & Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your Expedoo account and all associated data at any time — right from inside the app. No web form or email request needed.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Expedoo and go to the Account tab
  2. Tap the trash icon next to your email address
  3. Confirm by tapping Send email
  4. Open the email and tap Confirm deletion
Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All data is removed within 24 hours of confirmation.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your email address and account credentials
  • All trip records
  • All fuel entries
  • Account settings and preferences

Need Help?

If you have trouble deleting your account, email support@expedoo.top and we'll take care of it for you.
