Common Questions

How do I delete my account?
Go to the Account tab → tap the trash icon next to your email → confirm via the email link. Full steps: Delete Account.

My data isn't syncing
Make sure you have an internet connection — data syncs automatically when connected. If the issue persists, sign out and back in.

I didn't receive my login code
Check your spam folder. The email comes from noreply@expedoo.top. If it still doesn't arrive, try again after 60 seconds.

How do I report a bug?
Email support@expedoo.top with a description of the issue and your device model.
